Miny-Mo

A touch-enabled web page for picking who goes first

Scan or click the code below to launch.

Get people to touch and hold on the screen (up to the multi-touch limit of the device).

The page will eeny-meeny-mino-mo and highlight in green the Chosen One.

The count will restart if someone adds or removes a finger.

Once there is a Chosen One, removing all fingers resets.
